We are excited to share that [Sina Rahimi](/team/rahimi/) successfully defended his PhD on January 9, 2026.

Sina's dissertation, *Integrated Microclimate Simulation: A Novel Approach Towards Simulating Urban Microclimate*, reflects years of work on urban microclimate modeling, outdoor thermal comfort, and simulation tools that support sustainable design research.

This milestone also marks an important contribution to the development of [Eddy3D](https://www.eddy3d.com/) and to our broader research on urban physics and design-relevant environmental simulation.

Congratulations to Sina on this major achievement, and thank you to everyone who supported him throughout the process.
